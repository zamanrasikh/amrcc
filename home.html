Company Overview

Abrar & Mahmoodi Road & Construction Company (AMRCC), established in 2019, is a leading Afghan private construction firm specializing in roads, buildings, and infrastructure projects. We are committed to quality, safety, and empowering Afghan professionals. Our services include road construction, administrative buildings, water systems, and electrical infrastructure. With strong legal and UNGM registrations, we ensure transparency and accountability in all projects. Our mission focuses on sustainable growth, innovation, and community empowerment across Afghanistan.
